Vegetarian

A vegetarian diet does not include any meat, poultry, or seafood. It is a meal plan made up of foods that come mostly from plants. These include: Vegetables, Fruits, Whole grains, Legumes, Seeds and Nuts

Non Vegetarian

Non-veg diet includes meat, fish and sea food. This type of food is rich in protein and fats. Non veg foods contain B complex like B12, which is not found in veg foods. Non-veg diet has few disadvantages too, as it is high in calorie and lacks fibre.

Vegan

A vegan diet is based on plants such as vegetables, grains, nuts, fruits and foods made from plants. Vegans do not eat foods that come from animals, including dairy products and eggs.
